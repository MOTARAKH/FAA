Audit & Assurance


Business environment and regulatory requirements are becoming rapidly changing challenges. Audit and Assurance services promote confidence and trust of all stakeholders about the current position of an organization, and they are powerful indicators that provide insight about the future of this organization. FAA is committed to help you to pass these challenges and maintain sustainable growth, and simultaneously preserving the public interest. During the audit, we focus on the evaluation of the systems, procedures and practices from which the data is being generated, the quality, security, accessibility, reliability, accuracy, completeness and authenticity of the data and its relevance, timely availability and utility to management.

Samples of Audit & Assurance services that we offer:

  • External Audit of Financial Statement.
  • Independent Review & Compilation of Financial Statements.
  • Agreed Upon Procedures.
  • Special Purpose Audit (Audit of prospective financial information).
  • Financial reporting, and IFRS advice.
  • Corporate Governance Reviews.
  • Fraud Audit.
  • Reports on Internal Controls.
  • IT system reviews.
