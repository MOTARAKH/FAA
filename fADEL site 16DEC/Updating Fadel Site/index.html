About us

FAA was founded in 2004, and has built its reputation as a trusted effective and efficient professional services firm in Lebanon.We guide our clients through the complexity of their processes in a straightforward compliant and uncomplicated way to keep them focused on their businesses.  We offer our services to our clients in the  middle  market from  startups to fast-growing,  restructuring to expansion, to transition. Our services are growing in different industries not limited to Health-Care, Education, Services, Distribution, Manufacturing, Real-Estate, Construction, and Trade.

We are committed to our duties to clients, our ethics of professionalism, confidentiality, integrity, objectivity, serving the public interest, and to our standards of excellence, digitalization and internationalization. We offer assurance and related services, management consulting services, and tax services.

OUR VISION

We want to be the most trusted auditors, and advisors to our clients helping them succeed in their business, while maintaining the highest ethical standards and serving the public interest.

OUR MISSION

As an organization of professionals, we are here to use our resources of skills, knowledge, expertise, and network to understand the businesses and processes of our clients of corporates, government, and community organizations to help them develop a better understanding of their business needs, and to provide them with the services that help them achieve their goals in a straightforward and uncomplicated way, while always maintaining excellence in our values and standards.

OUR VALUES

Every task and decision of our mission is ruled by our values. Our values shape our culture and strategy. We perform our duties to our clients in their best interest with ownership, integrity, due care and diligence by providing them with agile and innovative solutions to earn their trust and satisfaction. Within our practices, we always strive to excellence, we follow the highest ethical standards, and we preserve the public interest. We commit ourselves to continuous self-development to accompany the advancement in technology and the development in laws and regulations.

Our values are our SUCCESS factors:

- Sincerity - Urgency - Commitment - Creativity - Excellence - Speed.
